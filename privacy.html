A short privacy notice.

This site is a personal website. It collects the minimum needed to know that it is being read, and nothing more.

What is collected

If you accept the cookie banner, this site loads Google Analytics 4 to record anonymous, aggregated visit statistics — page views, approximate country, the device category, and the page you came from. IP addresses are anonymized by configuration. No personally identifying information is stored. If you decline, the analytics script is not loaded at all, and nothing is sent.

What is not collected

No marketing pixels. No ad retargeting. No Facebook Pixel, no LinkedIn Insight Tag, no third-party trackers beyond Google Analytics. The zcal embed on the Contact page is loaded from zcal.co — its data handling is governed by zcal's privacy policy.

Cookies

Your consent choice is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key st_consent_v1. Google Analytics, if loaded, sets its own cookies (_ga, _ga_<id>). Clearing your browser storage resets both.

How to opt out

Clear the st_consent_v1 value in your browser's site storage and reload — the banner will show again. Or use a browser extension that blocks Google Analytics. Or enable Do Not Track in your browser; while this site doesn't parse that signal directly, most privacy extensions do.

Your rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) and similar laws in the EU and UK, you can request a copy of any personal data held about you, or ask for it to be deleted. Since this site doesn't store personal data beyond anonymous GA4 aggregates, there is usually nothing to return. If you want to be sure, email contact@saurabhtripathi.in.

Hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log request metadata (IP, user agent) as part of normal CDN operation. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Changes

If this notice changes in any material way, the version below will be bumped.
