01
Fit

Who the work is for, and who it isn't.

Who it is for

Founders, owners, retainer-minded operators.

  • Founders and owners who want a long-term partner, not a short-term vendor.
  • Businesses that have some traction, a real product, and a real budget.
  • Startups, SMBs, clinics, consultancies, D2C brands.
  • NGOs doing serious work.
  • Teams who want the marketing to match the quality of what they sell.
Who it is not for

Content farms, yes-men, broken funnels.

  • Thirty reels a month with no strategy behind them.
  • Anyone looking for an agency that says yes to everything.
  • Running ads while the website is broken and the offer is unclear.
  • One-shot engagements with no intent to build.
  • Briefs that are really a prayer for a miracle.
02
How it's different

Four operating choices that shape the work.

01 · Ownership

Saurabh runs the work.

Every client has his eyes on their account. No account-manager relay race, no bait-and-switch where the senior pitches and the intern delivers.

02 · Craft

Engineer first, marketer second.

Ten years in, the CS degree still shows up. Tracking, analytics, site speed, technical SEO, automation. Marketing that connects to what's happening on the product side.

03 · Model

Built for retainers.

Opus Momentum does not scale by adding new logos every month. It scales by keeping the ones it has. That only works if the work moves numbers, so it does.

04 · Range

Cross-industry pattern recognition.

Fintech, dentistry, skincare, NGOs, visa consulting. Experience that comes from working across sectors — not running the same playbook on repeat.
